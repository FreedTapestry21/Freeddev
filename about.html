About me

Hey there, I am Freed.
I'm a 15 year old bisexual human who loves technology.
I do a lot with programming and tinkering with software and electronics.

I do know some of the programming languages out there, like: Python and a bit of Javascript.
And I mean, HTML and CSS aren't really programming languages, but I do know those as well.
There are also some programming languages which I am still in the process of learning like Rust and C.

Oh yeah and not to forget, I am obsessed with Linux and smaller electronics, like Raspberry Pi's or Arduino's.

You can always check out some of my projects at GitHub.

My timeline

of everything I've done with Technology.

2019

The year of portable operating systems.
I started tinkering with different operating systems.
Especially Windows and FreeDOS.
Back then I also discovered on how to boot Windows or FreeDOS from a USB drive.
I also had my first encounter with Linux on my first Raspberry Pi.

2020

The year of ...being home?
In this year I started making a lot of Minecraft servers.
Thanks to this I learned about port forwarding and a bit about infrastructure.
I also bought my first Arduino in this year, which I liked to tinker with.
And not to forget, I tried to make this exact website, which back then failed.

2021

The year of Raspberry Pi.
In January 2021 I kinda destroyed my previous Raspberry Pi so I bought a new one.
I used this Raspberry pi as my server. And it's still online untill this day.
I also did a lot of small little Arduino projects in this year.
For example, I made a GUI for my Arduino with a mouse cursor and all.
Sadly, I never really used it in any kind of larger project.

2022

The year of Linux.
Around October 2021 I tried to install Zorin OS onto an external drive with no success.
So January 2022 I tried again, and it worked.
I started with Zorin OS and then started distro hopping.
Eventually in June 2022 I stopped at Arch Linux where I'm still at to this day.
I also setup 2 more servers.
A Raspberry Pi 400, which I use for my VPN and personal Minecraft server.
And an old Acer Aspire 8943G, which I use for almost everything server related.
